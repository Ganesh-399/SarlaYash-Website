Our Guiding Foundation

The name SarlaYash is a tribute to Maa Sarla and Paa Yash. Their lives modelled integrity, empathy and a quiet commitment to learning. These are not just words in our philosophy deck — they are the reference points for what we say “yes” or “no” to as an organisation.

Principles that shape every program

  • Integrity: We design and deliver in a way that we can explain with pride to the next generation.
  • Empathy: We recognise that each learner brings a different story, pace and starting point.
  • Lifelong learning: Our work is to keep curiosity alive, not to close the book after a certificate.
  • Holistic growth: Skills, mindset and values move together — not in isolation.
