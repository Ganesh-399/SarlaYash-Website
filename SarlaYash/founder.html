Meet Our Founder – Kapil Narula

Kapil Narula, our founder, upholds the legacy of Maa Sarla and Paa Yash. Their principles of integrity, empathetic leadership, and knowledge guide SarlaYash Learning Solutions LLP—transforming timeless values into modern leadership solutions.

Visionary Leadership

With over two decades of experience, Kapil Narula has contributed to reshaping Fortune 500 organizations and advancing learning & development initiatives. His work champions innovative, human-centric approaches that enable people and teams to thrive.

Guiding Philosophy

Kapil believes true leadership cultivates conscious growth, inspired by the holistic philosophy of Maa Sarla and Paa Yash. Individuals are empowered to discover inner strengths and align with a collective purpose.

His philosophy emphasizes lifelong learning, with impact measured through positive transformation in lives, organizations, and communities.

“Our greatest legacy is the wisdom and values we instill in others—shaping a future where leadership is synonymous with service, integrity, and compassionate action.”
