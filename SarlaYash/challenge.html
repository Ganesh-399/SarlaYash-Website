SarlaYash Wall of Fame

Celebrating learners who demonstrate creativity, discipline, and values — where effort, intent, and growth matter more than perfection.

The Wall of Fame showcases learners who combine creativity, discipline and values in their work. Initiatives like the Halloween Creative Challenge celebrate effort, intent and improvement — not just perfection.

What makes our challenges different?

  • Clear, transparent evaluation parameters shared upfront.
  • Personalised feedback so every participant learns something concrete.
  • Recognition that honours growth, not only rank.

For us, every challenge is another way to practice what SarlaYash stands for: learning that is joyful, accountable and deeply human.
